Product information

Tonal cream, or tonalka, is a cosmetic product used to even out the tone of the face and hide skin imperfections. It has a cream-like texture and usually contains certain ingredients such as pigments, silicones, mineral oils and other moisturizing and nourishing ingredients. Foundation can have a different texture, from light to full coverage. Light foundation has a thinner texture and is intended for those who want to cover some skin imperfections and make the face fresh and natural. Full coverage foundation, on the other hand, has a thicker texture and is intended for those who want to hide more serious skin imperfections such as acne, scarring, hyperpigmentation, and other issues.
